About Me

Hello everyone, I'm KANAV ARORA, a B.Tech (CSE) student at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun. I completed my schooling at O.S.D.A.V. Public School in the Kaithal district of Haryana.

Right from a young age, I was driven by curiosity about how things worked. The world seemed like a vast playground of mysteries waiting to be unraveled, and I was determined to explore every nook and cranny. Passionate about learning, I spent countless hours exploring the internet and engaging in conversations with anyone willing to share knowledge.

Interestingly, while many chose to study computer science formally or because of trends, I took a different path. Computer science wasn't a subject in the academic sense; it was a realm of fascination that I explored independently.

I grew not only in knowledge but also in the ability to share and collaborate. The hunger for learning new things was not a solitary pursuit; it became a journey to grow with others. I found like-minded individuals, forming a community bound by the shared passion.

I have a firm belief in Steve Jobs's thought “The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do.” And yes I belong to that group of crazy people.
